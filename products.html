Primer

How to Use Our Products

  1. Cleanse & Moisturize
    Wash your face and apply your usual skincare (moisturizer, SPF if daytime), letting it fully absorb.

  2. Dispense Primer
    Put a small, pea-sized amount onto your hand.

  3. Apply
    Dab the primer onto the center of your face (nose, cheeks, forehead) and blend outward using your fingertips or a brush.

  4. Focus Areas
    Concentrate extra product on areas with large pores, fine lines, or oiliness.

  5. Let it Set
    Wait 1-2 minutes (or about 30 seconds) for the primer to dry and grip the skin before applying foundation.

Foundation

How to Use Our Products

  1. Choose Your Too
    Use a clean, damp makeup sponge (bounce it on your face) or a dense, soft brush (buff in circles) for the best blend.

  2. Dot & Blend
    Apply small dots to the center of your face (forehead, nose, cheeks, chin) and blend outwards, working from the middle to the edges.

  3. Blend Edges
    Don't forget your hairline, jawline, and neck to avoid harsh lines.

  4. Build Coverage
    Add more product only where needed, blending thoroughly for a natural look.

Concealer

How to Use Our Products

  1. Apply Foundation
    Apply foundation first if using, but avoid blending it fully under the eyes to prevent creasing when concealer goes on top.

  2. Target Blemishes
    Use a concealer that matches your foundation shade. Dab a tiny dot directly onto the spot and blend the edges with a brush or finger.

  3. Blend
    Gently pat and press with a damp sponge or small brush. Avoid dragging to keep product in place and prevent patchiness.

  4. Set
    Lightly dust translucent setting powder over the concealer with a fluffy brush to lock it in and prevent creasing.
